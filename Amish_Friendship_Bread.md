Amish Friendship Bread
======================


Ingredients
-----------

* 1 cup starter

* 3 eggs

* 1/2 cup milk

* 1 cup sugar

* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

* 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

* 1 large box vanilla pudding mix

* 2 teaspoon cinnamon

* 1/2 teaspoon salt

* 2 cups flour

* 1 cup oil or 1/2 cup oil and 1/2 cup applesauce

* 1 teaspoon baking soda


Directions
----------

1) Mix all togher.

2) Bake for an our at 325f.
