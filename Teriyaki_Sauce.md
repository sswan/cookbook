Teriyaki Sauce
==============

Ingredients
-----------

* 1/2 cup soy sauce

* 2 tablespoons rice vinegar

* 3 tablespoons mirin

* 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil

* 1/3 cup packed brown sugar

* 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

* 1/8 teaspoon ginger powder

* 1/2 teaspoon salt



Directions
----------

* Mix it all together
