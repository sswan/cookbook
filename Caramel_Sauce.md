Caramel Sauce
=============

Ingredients
-----------

* 1 cup brown sugar

* 1/2 cup butter

* 1/4 cup corn syrup

* 1 cup pecans


Directions
----------

* Put the brown sugar and the butter in a saucepan and bring to a boil.

* Once it is boiling, add the corn syrup and stir/whisk.

* Add pecans or put pecans on whatever you want and pour the sauce over it.
