Swedish Meatballs
=================

Ingredients
-----------

* 1 lb beef

* 1 egg beaten

* 1/4 cup bread crumbs

* 2 carotts grated

* 2 medium potatos grated

* 1 onion diced

* salt and peper


Directions
----------

1) Form balls and brown

2) Bake 325f for 30 minutes then melt cheese


Sauce
=====

Ingredients
-----------

* 2 tablespoons butter
 
* 2 tbs flour
 
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
 
* 1 cup milk
