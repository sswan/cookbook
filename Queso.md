Queso
=====


Ingredients
-----------

* 1 large box Velveeta cheese

* 2 cans cream of mushroom soup

* 1 can cheddar cheese soup

* 1 container/tub of Bueno green chili, drained

* 1 roll of Jimmy Dean original sausage

* 1/2 - 3/4 cup milk


Ingredients
-----------

Cook and drain sausage. Crumble sausage pieces and add to crockpot. Cube Velveeta cheese and add soups and thawed/drained chile to crockpot. Mix and add milk until desired consistency is reached. Heat on low for 2 hours and serve with chips.
