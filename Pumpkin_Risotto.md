Pumpkin Risotto
===============


Ingredients
-----------

* 1 tablespoon butter

* 1 small onion, finely chopped

* 2 cloves garlic, minced

* 1 cup arborio rice

* 1/2 cup dry white wine OR 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar

* 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warmed

* 1 cup pumpkin puree (not pie filling)

* 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (or more to taste)

* 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

* salt and pepper to taste (roughly 1/4 teaspoon each)

* 1 tablespoon chopped fresh sage (or 1 teaspoon dried)

* optional: chopped walnuts and/or pecans, toasted pumpkin seeds


Directions
----------



* In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add onion and saute until soft and translucent, about 4-5 minutes.

* Stir in garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

* Add arborio rice and stir constantly for 1-2 minutes to toast the grains.

* Pour in the white wine and stir until mostly absorbed.

* Gradually add the warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Wait until the liquid is mostly absorbed before adding the next ladle. Continue this process for about 20 minutes.

* Once the rice is creamy and al dente, stir in the pumpkin puree and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

* Add the Parmesan cheese and chopped sage. Stir until the cheese is melted and incorporated.

* Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan, toasted pumpkin seeds, or fresh sage leaves if desired.
